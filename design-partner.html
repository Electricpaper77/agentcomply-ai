AgentComply AI Design Partner Packet

AgentComply AI is a pre-validation audit evidence layer for AI-agent actions in regulated workflows.

Request Design Partner Call Test Risk Engine

Synthetic demo only. No live customer data. No legal advice or compliance certification.

Audience

Who this is for

  • Compliance leaders
  • Risk leaders
  • CISOs
  • Security/platform engineers
  • AI governance teams

Workflows

Best-fit pilot workflows

  • Insurance claims
  • Regulated customer support
  • Customer communications using PII
  • Agent-triggered approvals
  • Refund/payment-adjacent workflows

Scope

Pilot scope

  • One workflow
  • One integration point
  • One audit schema
  • One small policy rule library
  • One audit-ready report
  • 4-8 week validation window

Inputs

What we need from a design partner

  • Named compliance/risk champion
  • Named technical/platform champion
  • Example workflow
  • Sample synthetic or sanitized event logs
  • Weekly feedback call
  • Permission to learn from the workflow, not public logo usage

Outputs

What design partners get

  • Early input into product direction
  • Custom audit evidence schema for one workflow
  • Synthetic or sanitized pilot report
  • Risk decision logic review
  • Clear findings summary

Boundaries

What this is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not compliance certification
  • Not SOC 2 or HIPAA certified
  • Not production enforcement
  • Not connected to live customer data unless separately scoped
  • Not a replacement for compliance/legal/auditor judgment

Success criteria

Pilot success criteria

Can we prove what the agent did?

Can we prove what data it touched?

Can we show which policy applied?

Can compliance understand the report?

Can engineering integrate without heavy workflow disruption?

Next step

The design-partner conversation is focused on learning which audit evidence is useful, inspectable, and realistic for one regulated AI-agent workflow.
